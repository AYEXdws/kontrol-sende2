Understanding Addiction

What is Addiction?

Addiction is a complex condition, a brain disorder that is manifested by compulsive substance use, despite harmful consequences.

Signs of Addiction

  • Increased tolerance
  • Withdrawal symptoms
  • Loss of control
  • Neglecting responsibilities
