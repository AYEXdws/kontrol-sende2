Understanding Addiction

What is Addiction?

Addiction is a complex condition, a brain disorder that is manifested by compulsive substance use despite harmful consequences. It affects the brain's reward, motivation, and memory functions.

Types of Addiction

There are various types of addiction including behavioral addictions such as gambling addiction, internet addiction, and food addiction. Each type has its own unique challenges.

Ways to Combat Addiction

Combatting addiction requires a multifaceted approach, including therapy, support groups, and lifestyle changes. It is essential to seek help and understand that recovery is possible.
